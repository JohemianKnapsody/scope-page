The OpenFlexure Microscope

The OpenFlexure Microscope is an automated tissue scanner which can be built anywhere in the world.

The Challenge

Digital microscopy, and especially automated tissue scanners, are conventionally extremely expensive and difficult to maintain. In many areas of the world, scanners are either unsuitable for the budget, resources and training of the pathology lab, or entirely unavailable.
