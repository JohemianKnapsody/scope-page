Our Seminars

Pathology is a collaborative field, and there's no substitute for interative learning. Join our sessions to discuss, suggest, learn, teach and diagnose dozens of cases taken from clinics around the world.

Our approach

Each ScOPE seminar is led by the attendees. The lecturer will share digital images of their most educational cases before the seminar, allowing every attendee to study and annotate the sample. Each case will be presented with patient history and symptoms, mirroring the diagnostic process. Attendees are invited to comment and ask questions during each seminar, with a truly interative learning experience.
