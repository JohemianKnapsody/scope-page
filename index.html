Revolutionary Pathology Education

Pathology is one of the most essential medical fields, and one of the most affected by disparities in resources and region. The Open Pathology School hosts regular training sessions on digital slides. The slides have been scanned from pathology departments around the world, on a tissue scanner that has been built and used in over 60 countries.
